The Monster Kings Whitepaper

Introduction

The Monster Kings is a world where all the NFTs are created by you. Among the thousands of possibilities, find what you like and what is valuable. Having a monster allows you to start adventures.

You can also raise your monsters during your adventures to make them rarer and more powerful. You can also form your own team of monsters and use the power of spells to defeat powerful opponents.

Of course, the rarer and more powerful your monsters are, the more money you will earn.

Rich gameplay, high player rewards, low participation threshold and highly rewarding group battles ...... are waiting for you to challenge.

NFT Overview

In the world of The Monster Kings, the main NFTs are monsters.

Each monster consists of 21 parts, each with an average of dozens of different features, and hundreds of colours. This gives each created monster a great uniqueness.

The probability of appearance of each feature depends on their respective rarity. And the colours affects the monster's combat attributes in some ways. Of course, the fighting ability of a monster depends mainly on its grade and level.

The rarity and fighting ability of the monster in turn affects the amount of income in many ways. They are the ticket to all competitions.

Generating Rules

Each monster is made up of 21 parts, but not every monster has all 21 parts, depending on its grade. The higher the grade, the more parts will grow.

There are 5 different grades of monsters. The different grades correspond to the probability of different rare parts appearing.

Each monster has a total of 10 levels and each level increase grows at least one part.

When level 10 is reached, the monster completes the growth process and its rarity and combat ability will no longer change.

Full level monsters that have completed the growth process can participate in games such as battle ranking and can start breeding.

Rarity

The rarity of a monster is the sum of the rarity of all its parts.

The higher the rarity of the monster, the more special features it has, and then the more valuable it is to collect.

Monsters with above average rarity in the same grade will be given special marks.
A silver medal indicates that the monster's rarity is higher than the average score of the same grade.
A gold medal indicates that the monster's rarity is well above the average score of the same grade.

Generations

All monsters can reproduce for a maximum of 7 generations.

Only the 10,000 monsters sold in the pre-order phase are Generation 0. Each Generation 0 monster is level 10 (maximum level).

In the future, all forms of the monster purchased through the official blind box will be Generation 1.

When breeding, two monsters give birth to a baby whose generation is the maximum of the parents' generation plus one.

Battle

Each adult monster (level 10) can participate in any type of battle game, such as Treasure Map.

In the battle between individual monsters, the grade determines the base combat attribute value of the monster, the rarity affects the defence value, and the colour deviation affects the attack power of the monster.

Simply put, the higher the grade, the stronger the overall ability. On the basis of the same grade, the higher the rarity, the stronger the defence ability. The stronger the combat ability, the higher the attack power.

Presales

Only Generation 0 monsters will be sold in the pre-sale, and only 10,000 will be available in total.

All Generation 0 monsters are sold using player creation. This means that the avatar of each Generation 0 monster is created and chosen by the player.

After the pre-sale ends, no more Generation 0 monsters will be available for sale.

The highest grade a monster can reach in pre-sale is Excellent. And Excellent is the necessary foundation for breeding Perfect monsters.

It is worth noting that all monsters sold during the pre-sale are full level monsters. After the pre-sale ends, players will only be able to buy monster eggs in the store or buy monsters from other players.

Game Overview

The core gameplay revolves around raising monsters, adventuring and winning races.

In the game, luck is just as important as intelligence. But overall, the more quests you complete each day, and the more times you spin the Lucky Roulette, and help the monsters grow better and faster, the more you will gain.

Players can go and steal other people's gold, and of course they can strengthen their defence to prevent others from stealing their own gold.

Of course, we also need to pay attention to the state of the monsters, they will become unhappy when they are hungry, and their ability to fight will be reduced.

Players can also collect component features from other monsters during the adventure, which can help them determine the direction of their evolution as they grow.

Lucky Roulette

The Lucky Roulette is the most central part of the entire game. Players can trigger an event by putting in energy.

Every event can be different. For example, pick up a bag of gold, strengthen the camp's defences, steal other players' gold, complete a monster forest adventure, go head-to-head with other players, absorb other monsters' features, feed your own monsters, and so on.

If you want to play a little faster, you can put more energy into a turn and then you will get more rewards.

Fostering

Once each monster egg hatches into a monster, it can grow by feeding magic power. Magic power bottles can be obtained by completing quests or winning battles.

Each time a monster levels up, a new body part grows, along with its rarity and combat ability. Once a monster has grown to level 10 it can no longer be fed magic and its rarity and combat ability will no longer be able to increase.

Monsters' hunger level increases after completing a battle and when they are very hungry, their mood deteriorates, which causes them to be less capable in battle. So players need to pay attention to this and feed them monster cookies instantly.

Each monster has different skills depending on the base colour. These skills are not very useful in individual battles, but are useful in team battles.

Breeding

Once a monster reaches level 10, it can lay a egg with another level 10 monster, if neither of the two monsters reach generation 7.

The monster egg may be in a higher grade than its parents, but in most cases, it is the same as the parents.

Monster eggs hatch and become level 1 monsters. As they grow, almost half of their body parts will inherit their parents' features. Players can also invest features collected from other monsters as they level up, thus increasing the probability of a specific feature being selected.

The young monsters can continue to breed the next generation when they grow up. However, if it is already in its 7th generation, then it will not be able to continue breeding offspring.

Tasks

Completing tasks is the way to get higher rewards.

There are randomly generated tasks in the game every day, and you can get double rewards for completing them consecutively.

It should be noted that all tasks are timed and need to be completed within the specified time, if the time is exceeded, the task will fail.

Adventure

The game has 10 base challenges that accompany the player's growth process.

Each successful challenge will correspond to the opening of a new game feature. For example, when the first challenge is successful, the in-game store will be opened. After the third successful challenge, the formation panel will be opened. After the sixth successful challenge, the monster breeding will be opened. After the eighth successful challenge, the ranking that can win MNT will be opened. And so on.

After players complete all the basic challenges, it will not only open all the game play, but the pass reward will also get a MNT treasure chest.

Team Battles

Monsters are fought in teams, which means players need to use their intelligence to arrange their squads in a more effective way to make the most of the monsters' collaborative value.

Monsters with the same skill will combine their skills into a powerful new skill if they stand in a line. However, different skills will also combine to create powerful new skills if placed in specific locations.

Players can explore more ways to combine skills to better play the monsters' combat abilities.

Shops

The in-game store mainly uses gold and MNT as the settlement currency and sells some common props in the game.

The items for sale are mainly energy, monster cookies, feature scrolls, breeding stones and defensive shields.

Rare and special props within the game, as well as monsters, will be sold by players at auction.

Rankings

The ranking is divided into player battle ranking, and pet ranking. Pets are the monsters owned by the players.

Each battle between players increases the ranking points of the winning side and decreases the points of the losing side.

The ranking of pets, on the other hand, is a rarity ranking of all full-level monsters and do not need to be involved in combat.

There will be separate reward treasure chests for ranking, and each time the countdown ends, a generous reward will be given to the top ten players or monster owners.
